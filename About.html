Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: What types of construction materials do you supply? +
We supply a wide range of construction materials including cement, steel, bricks, sand, aggregates, wood, and more.
Q2: Do you deliver to all locations? +
We provide delivery services to most areas. During checkout, you can confirm if your location is covered.
Q3: Can I get bulk orders? +
Yes! We handle bulk orders for large construction projects and offer competitive pricing.
Q4: How can I pay for my order? +
We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, online banking, and cash on delivery.
Q5: Do you offer technical support or product guidance? +
Absolutely! Our experts can help you choose materials and understand proper usage.
Q6: Are your materials certified for quality? +
Yes, all our products meet industry standards for quality, safety, and durability.
Q7: How do I track my order? +
You'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS after confirming your order.
